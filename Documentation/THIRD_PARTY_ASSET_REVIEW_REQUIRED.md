# Third-Party Asset Review Required

The starter-garage package contains models and textures supplied by the project
owner from multiple third-party packs.

Before any public or commercial build:

1. Identify the original source of every included asset.
2. Record its license.
3. Record required attribution.
4. Confirm modification rights.
5. Confirm commercial-use rights.
6. Confirm whether redistributing source FBX and texture files is permitted.
7. Replace any asset whose terms are unclear.

This development package does not certify those rights.
