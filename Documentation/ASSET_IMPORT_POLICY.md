# Asset Import Policy

## Approved visual direction

Use realistic and semi-realistic assets matching the gritty industrial art direction.

## Asset review checklist

Before an asset enters the live project:

- Confirm license and commercial-use rights.
- Record source and creator.
- Confirm physical scale.
- Correct pivot and transforms.
- Rebuild URP materials.
- Remove redundant textures.
- Reduce oversized textures.
- Add simple colliders.
- Add LODs when repeated or high-poly.
- Confirm prefab naming.
- Confirm placement-system compatibility.
- Confirm electrical or interaction requirements.
- Test in the main gameplay scene.

## Usually approved

- Cargo containers
- Railway props
- Warehouses
- Industrial lighting
- CCTV cameras
- Keypads
- Breaker panels
- Old furniture
- Office furniture
- Realistic vehicles
- Dumpsters and trash bags
- Pallets and cargo crates
- Mid-detail vegetation with LODs

## Usually excluded from close-range final art

- PSX buildings
- Strongly stylized low-poly city props
- High-poly vegetation without LODs
- Dense scan ivy
- Duplicate high/mid/low asset packages loaded simultaneously
- Assets with no practical license record
