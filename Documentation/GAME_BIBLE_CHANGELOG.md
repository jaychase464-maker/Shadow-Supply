## 1.0.0 — 2026-07-14

**Status:** Approved initial foundation

### Established

- Shadow Supply as a relationship-driven open-world criminal business simulator
- Low-level counterfeiter starting premise
- 300-hour persistent-save target
- Relationship-first unlock structure
- Overall, district, industry, NPC, and faction reputation layers
- Rapport, trust, respect, loyalty, and fear relationship dimensions
- NPC memory and persistent consequences
- Employee recruitment through relationships
- Supplier and buyer characters rather than vending-machine NPCs
- Branching faction routes
- No universal perfect playthrough
- Dynamic post-story sandbox
- Materially different new worlds
- Manual interactive production for every recipe
- Physical power, logistics, storage, deliveries, and properties
- Dirty and clean cash
- Island-metropolis map structure
- Natural water and terrain boundaries
- Main, eastern, western, and southern landmasses
- Working district identities
- Dynamic district and faction state
- Save-schema and stable-ID preservation requirements
- Bible amendment and versioning policy
