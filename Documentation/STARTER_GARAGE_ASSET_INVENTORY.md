## Optimization

- Structural and ground textures are capped at 2K
- Street-prop texture is capped at 1K
- Redundant bump, cavity, gloss, displacement, specular, and AO maps are not
  duplicated in this milestone package
- Large source collections remain outside the scene
- Generated prefabs receive simple gameplay colliders
