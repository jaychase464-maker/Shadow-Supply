# v0.9.1 — Counterfeit Press Clarity Hotfix

The block prototype made it unclear which objects were materials and which
were work areas.

## Immediate correction

During step 1:

- The small yellow block at the lower-left is `Blank Note Stock`.
- The large orange square at the upper-left is the `Paper Tray`.

Drag the yellow block onto the orange square.

## Hotfix changes

- Adds `DRAG THIS` above the exact required material.
- Adds `DROP HERE` above the exact destination.
- Adds `HOLD THIS` above PRINT.
- Adds `CLICK THIS` above the toolkit and SEAL.
- Adds a permanent process legend.
- Adds screen markers over the required object and target.
- Clicking the wrong material only shows a hint.
- Clicking PRINT, SEAL, or the toolkit out of sequence only shows a hint.
- Actually dropping a material in the wrong work area still counts as a
  production mistake.

## Installation

1. Exit Play Mode.
2. Close Unity.
3. Extract into the Unity project root.
4. Replace all prompted files.
5. Reopen Unity and wait for compilation.

Do not rerun the Milestone 9A setup menu.
